# Warm Response — (Not a Lead Yet)

- Warm Response - Prospect replied "yes" to a cold email or cold SMS. No phone call has occurred yet.

# Not Contacted / Not Qualified

- New Leads - Leads that are brand new and no attempt to contact has been made
- Day 1-2 - Contacts attempted but have not been qualified yet - Day 1-2 since lead came in
- Day 3-14 - Contacts attempted but have not been qualified yet - Day 3-14 since lead came in
- Day 15-30 - Contacts attempted but have not been qualified yet - Day 15-30 since lead came in
- Cold - Contacts attempted but have not been qualified yet - Day 30+ since lead came in

# Disqualified Leads — Terminal (no future contact)

- Dispo: Not a Fit - Property or Owner are not a fit for our company and we do not want it
- Dispo: No Longer Own - Lead already sold property
- Dispo: Purchased - We purchased the property
- Dispo: DNC - Lead wants to be added to our Do Not Contact list

# Disqualified Leads — Re-Engage (long-term drip)

- Dispo: No Motivation - Lead has no motivation to sell
- Dispo: Wants Retail - Lead wants retail / full market value
- Dispo: On MLS - Property is on the MLS / Market with a Realtor
- Dispo: Lead Declined - Lead declined to sell or our offer and deal wasn't close enough to put in "nurture" stage

# Qualified Leads

- Due Diligence - We are researching the property after we qualify but before we make an offer
- Make Offer - We are ready to make offer on property
- Negotiations - Made offer and lead did not accept so we are negotiating
- Contract Sent - Lead agreed to sell and we sent contract
- Under Contract - Lead signed a contract
- Nurture - Qualified leads that we couldn't close so we want to nurture for a future deal
