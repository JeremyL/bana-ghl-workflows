## Day 0–30 — Active Follow-Up

- **Section guide:**
  - The 30-day structured pursuit from attempted first touch to cold.
  - Tone shifts from direct (Day 0) to empathetic and permission-based (Day 30).
  - All sources get the same cadence after Day 0.
  - NO Qualifying Call Yet. 
    - Day 0 through 30 is all an attempt to have that first qualifying call
    - Both for the lead manager and the acquisition manager, depending on the lead source.
  - Once qualifying call has been had, he lead will exit all day zero through 30 automations upon stage change
- **Day 0 — Speed to Lead**
  - **Cold Outbound** (Cold SMS / Cold Call)
    - 120s after entry · SMS · CO-SMS-00
      - Cold intro — this is AGENT NAME, lets know call is coming
    - ~1hr later > Missed-call SMS if no call logged · SMS · CO-SMS-00A
      - Missed call notice — says we tried calling, leaves callback number, need to verify some things about property
  - **Inbound** (Website / VAPI / Referral)
    - 120s after entry · SMS · IN-SMS-00
      - Inbound acknowledgment — references that their info just came through, lets know call is coming
    - ~1hr later > Missed-call SMS if no call logged · SMS · IN-SMS-00A
      - Missed call notice — framed for inbound leads, leaves callback number, need to verify some things about property
  - **Direct Mail**
    - 120s after entry · SMS · DM-SMS-00
      - Letter follow-up — references the mailer they received, lets know call is coming
    - ~1hr later > Missed-call SMS if no call logged · SMS · DM-SMS-00A
      - Missed call notice — references the letter, leaves callback number, need to verify some things about property
- **Day 1–2 — Heavy Pursuit (2x/day)**
  - Day 1, Morning · SMS · NL-SMS-01
    - Warm opener —
  - Day 1, Afternoon · SMS · NL-SMS-07
    - Missed call follow-up —
  - Day 2, Morning · Email · NL-EMAIL-01
    - First email —
  - Day 2, Afternoon · SMS · NL-SMS-02
    - Soft nudge —
- **Day 3–10 — Daily Rotation (1x/day)**
  - Day 4 · SMS · NL-SMS-10
    - Casual value hint — something came up about their property, creates curiosity
  - Day 5 · Email · NL-EMAIL-05
    - Social proof angle — working on deals in their county, their property keeps coming up
  - Day 7 · Email · NL-EMAIL-02
    - Right person check — making sure we're reaching the actual owner
  - Day 8 · SMS · NL-SMS-03
    - Direct timing question —
  - Day 10 · SMS · NL-SMS-08
    - Permission to back off — not trying to bug you, would a conversation even make sense?
- **Day 11–30 — Winding Down (every 2–3 days)**
  - Day 11 · SMS · NL-SMS-04
    - Re-engage — where should we go from here?
  - Day 14 · RVM · NL-RVM-01
    - Open loop — going through properties in their area, had a question (doesn't say what)
  - Day 15 · Email · NL-EMAIL-03
    - Timing empathy — sometimes timing isn't right, has anything changed?
  - Day 17 · SMS · NL-SMS-09
    - Offer tease — would it help if I just sent you a number?
  - Day 20 · RVM · NL-RVM-02
    - Open loop — got something to run by them (vague on purpose)
  - Day 22 · SMS · NL-SMS-05
    - Curious question — what would need to happen for selling to make sense?
  - Day 27 · RVM · NL-RVM-03
    - Wrap-up hook — before I close this out, wanted to connect
  - Day 29 · Email · NL-EMAIL-04
    - Long-game setup — moving you to the back of my list, last heads up
  - Day 30 · SMS · NL-SMS-06
    - 30-day close — closing out notes, you've got my number

## Cold — Cold Lead Monthly Drip

- **Section guide:**
  - Fully automated.
  - Lead didn't respond in 30 days or was marked Lost (with reason).
  - Tone is low-pressure, curious, and spaced out.
  - Goal is to catch them when timing changes.
  - After Month 3, leads enter the Long-Term Quarterly drip.
- **Cold Monthly (Months 1–3)**
  - Day 30 · SMS · COLD-SMS-01
    - Circle back — did you ever figure out what you wanted to do?
  - Day 44 · Email · COLD-EMAIL-01
    - Proximity trigger — we closed a deal nearby, reminded me of yours
  - Day 58 · SMS · COLD-SMS-02
    - Tax pain point — are you paying taxes and wondering if it's worth holding?
  - Day 72 · Email · COLD-EMAIL-02
    - Area activity — doing more deals in your area, would you reconsider?
  - Day 86 · SMS · COLD-SMS-03
    - Casual check-in — your property still on your mind, or have you moved on?
  - Day 100 · Email · COLD-EMAIL-03
    - Soft close — is selling completely off the table or just not the right time?

## Nurture — Warm Lead Monthly Drip

- **Section guide:**
  - Fully automated.
  - Lead engaged or qualified but the deal stalled.
  - Tone is warmer and relationship-based — we've already talked.
  - Goal is to re-activate when circumstances change.
  - After Month 3, leads enter the Long-Term Quarterly drip.
- **Nurture Monthly (Months 1–3)**
  - Month 1 · SMS · NUR-SMS-01
    - Post-conversation check — thinking about our talk, has anything shifted?
  - Month 2 · Email · NUR-EMAIL-01
    - Timing empathy — last time we spoke timing wasn't right, has anything changed?
  - Month 3 · SMS · NUR-SMS-02
    - Reconnect ask — where do things stand, would love to reconnect if it makes sense

## Long-Term — Quarterly Drip

- **Section guide:**
  - Fully automated. Shared drip — both Cold and Nurture leads feed into this after their monthly phase.
  - Q1–Q4 plays twice (24 months total), then stops. No infinite loop.
  - Tone is middle-ground — curious, low-pressure, never references prior conversations.
  - Goal is extremely light touch at near-zero cost until timing changes or we give up.
- **Long-Term Quarterly (Month 4–28, Q1–Q4 plays twice then stops)**
  - Q1 · SMS · LTQ-SMS-01
    - Simple re-ask — did you decide what to do with the property?
  - Q1 · Email · LTQ-EMAIL-01
    - Still active — we're still buying in your area, worth a conversation?
  - Q2 · SMS · LTQ-SMS-02
    - Social proof — just picked up another property in your county
  - Q2 · Email · LTQ-EMAIL-02
    - Area activity — working with landowners nearby, your property came up
  - Q3 · SMS · LTQ-SMS-03
    - Assumption reversal — figured you decided to keep it, is that right?
  - Q3 · Email · LTQ-EMAIL-03
    - File cleanup — had your property in my notes for a while, should I keep it?
  - Q4 · SMS · LTQ-SMS-04
    - Honest question — what's keeping you from selling?
  - Q4 · Email · LTQ-EMAIL-04
    - Opt-out offer — should I keep you on my list or stop reaching out?

## Missed Call Text-Back

- **Section guide:**
  - Auto-reply when a known contact calls and we miss it.
  - Quick acknowledgment to keep the conversation alive.
- 2 min after missed inbound call · SMS · MC-SMS-01
  - Acknowledgment — saw you called, I'll try you back or text me here
